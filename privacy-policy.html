Privacy Policy

App name: Beth Notes

Developer contact: collinskimathi49@gmail.com

Effective date: March 15, 2026

Beth Notes is a personal notes app. We respect your privacy and keep data on your device.

Information We Collect

We do not require an account. Notes, titles, and tags are stored locally on your device. We do not collect or transmit your notes to our servers.

Voice Input

If you use voice input, your device's speech recognition service may process audio to generate a transcript. We do not store audio recordings, and we do not receive or share the audio or transcript outside your device.

Permissions

Microphone access is used only when you tap the mic button for voice input.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transmit personal data to third parties.

Data Retention and Deletion

You can delete notes in the app at any time. Uninstalling the app removes all local data stored by Beth Notes.

Children's Privacy

Beth Notes is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date at the top will be updated.
